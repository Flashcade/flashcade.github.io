About Flashcade

Flashcade is a non-profit fan project by Swumby (https://swumby.neocities.org), to make an arcade for everyone's favorite Macromedia and/or Adobe Flash games!
It's currently still being made, but we plan for the site to launch with at least 25 popular Flash games. We'll have a section for submitting games and more, so if this sounds nice to you, won't you be a cool dude or dudette and stay tuned for some updates?
Flashcade is strictly non-commercial. There'll be no ads, donations, or premium stuff. All the games we host belong to their own creators, and we will do our best to credit the creators when needed. If we host your game and you'd like it taken down, make an issue in this repository or shoot a message to Swumby at swumby(at)protonmail(dot)com. Your game will be removed immediately, no questions asked.

Other Details/Credits
